Is the Children's Pajamas safe?

Ivy City · Recalled Jan 16, 2025
No — the Children's Pajamas was recalled on Jan 16, 2025 due to a fire hazard. Does not meet flammability safety standards and can catch fire too easily. If you have this product: Stop your child from wearing or using this immediately. It does not meet flammability safety standards and can catch fire quickly. Return for a refund.
