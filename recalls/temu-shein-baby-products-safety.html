Are Temu & Shein baby products safe?

Recalled baby & kids items from ultra-cheap import sellers — what to check

Ultra-cheap baby items from Temu and Shein often ship directly from overseas sellers and may not meet U.S. safety standards. TinySafe tracks 13 baby and kids products sold through Temu or Shein that were recalled — including loungers, crib bumpers, toys, and sleepwear, often for violating mandatory U.S. federal safety rules. Before buying baby products from these marketplaces, check them against the recalls below and confirm with the CPSC. A product not listed here is not confirmed safe.

Common questions

Are baby products from Temu and Shein safe?
Some are, but baby and kids items from Temu and Shein — often shipped directly from overseas sellers — have been recalled, frequently for violating mandatory U.S. safety standards. Check a specific product against official CPSC recalls before buying.
Why do Temu and Shein baby products get recalled?
The most common reason is failing to meet mandatory U.S. federal safety standards — such as banned small parts, non-compliant infant sleep products, or flammable sleepwear. Many ship directly from overseas sellers.
Which Temu/Shein baby products have been recalled?
TinySafe tracks baby and kids products sold through Temu or Shein that were recalled, grouped by hazard such as suffocation, choking, and magnets. The full list with official CPSC sources is on this page.
Is it safe to buy a baby lounger or crib bumper from Temu?
Several loungers and crib bumpers sold through these marketplaces have been recalled for suffocation risk, and padded crib bumpers are discouraged for infant sleep generally. Check the specific product against official recalls and follow safe-sleep guidance.
How do I check if a Temu or Shein product was recalled?
Find the product or brand on this page or in the TinySafe recall index. Each entry links to the official CPSC recall record so you can confirm the affected models.
