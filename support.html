Frequently asked

What is TinySafe?+

TinySafe helps parents keep track of baby product recalls. We check recall data from the FDA and CPSC every day and flag recalls that match the brands you follow. Ingredient analysis, grounded in established dermatological and pharmaceutical standards, is coming soon.

Is TinySafe free?+

Yes. You can browse recalls and follow brands for free, no account required for browsing. Signing in (with Apple or Google) unlocks personalized alerts for the brands you follow.

How do you protect my family's privacy?+

Your child's name and exact date of birth never leave your device — we never send them to our servers. We only use a general stage and age range to personalize what you see. You can read the full details in our Privacy Policy.

Why does TinySafe ask for my location?+

It's optional. If you share your location, we use only a general region to help make your recall alerts smarter over time. We never collect precise GPS coordinates, and the app works fully without it.

How do notifications work?+

When you follow a brand, we send a push notification when a new recall matches it. We check FDA and CPSC feeds every day. You can manage notifications anytime in your device Settings.

How do I delete my account?+

Open the app, go to Settings, and tap "Delete account." This permanently removes your account and data from our servers and wipes your local data. It can't be undone.

Where does the recall data come from?+

From official public sources: the U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA) and the U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC). TinySafe is for general awareness and isn't an official government source — always verify critical details with the manufacturer or the relevant authority.
