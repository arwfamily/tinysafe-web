What TinySafe does

Recalls from official sources, every day

We check recall data from the U.S. FDA and CPSC daily and bring it into one place — no more scattered government databases.

Curated for the 0–4 age range

You see baby and toddler products — formula, gear, toys, skincare — not unrelated items. The most serious recalls are sorted to the top.

Add your brands

Tell us the brands you use, and recalls that match them are flagged for you. Get a push notification when a new one matches.

Plain language, not legalese

Each recall is summarized in clear terms: what the product is, what the hazard is, and what to do — with a link to the official source.

Your information stays yours

Your child's name and date of birth never leave your device — we never send them to our servers. We collect as little as possible, we don't sell your data, and you can delete your account and all associated data anytime from within the app.

You can browse all recalls without an account. Sign in only if you want personalized brand tracking.

TinySafe is an independent app built on publicly available government recall data. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by the FDA, CPSC, or any government agency. TinySafe helps you keep track of recalls but cannot guarantee that every recall is detected or that an alert will always arrive — always confirm details with the official source linked in each recall. The absence of a recall does not guarantee a product is safe.
