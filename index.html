Before Woojin was born, I researched everything and bought a high chair from a well-known brand — over $400 — trusting that a name like that had to be safe. Months later, I kept finding tiny screws on the floor. He tried to put one in his mouth.

Then one day, lifting him out after lunch, the seat came apart from the legs. He almost fell.

I searched — and found it had been recalled. If it can happen to me, it can happen to any parent. So I built TinySafe — to protect my son, and yours.

— Angela, mom to Woojin
